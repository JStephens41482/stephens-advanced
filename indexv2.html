About Us

Licensed and Built for DFW

Stephens Advanced LLC is a full-service fire suppression and safety company serving the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. We specialize in the inspection, testing, and maintenance of commercial kitchen hood suppression systems, portable fire extinguishers, clean agent systems, and emergency lighting.

We're licensed by the Texas State Fire Marshal's Office and report directly to local fire marshals through The Compliance Engine in applicable jurisdictions.

  • Texas State Fire Marshal Licensed
  • Full DFW metroplex coverage — 33+ cities
  • Brycer / TCE compliance reporting
  • 24/7 emergency service available
  • All major suppression system brands serviced
