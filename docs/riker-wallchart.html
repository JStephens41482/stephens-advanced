How To Give Riker Orders That Stick

These phrases tell Riker to REMEMBER a rule permanently:
that's an order   I'm ordering you to…   from now on…   never do X   always do X   stop doing X   going forward…   make it a rule that…

When Riker hears one of these from you (via SMS or field app), it writes a STANDING ORDER to memory at priority 10, permanent, and follows it from then on. Reply should come back as "Locked in."

To revoke a rule: cancel that rule, forget that order, or that rule no longer applies. No other phrasing unwinds it.

The Approval Gate

When a customer proposes a time (via website / SMS / email), Riker texts you first:

CONFIRM? Taqueria La Estrella (via sms_customer)
2026-04-23 10:00 (1.5hr)
suppression, extinguishers
Arlington

Reply YES to approve, NO to reject, or suggest alt like "Tue 2pm"

Reply options

  • YES / Y / OK / do it → books the job, texts/emails the customer the confirmation
  • NO / N / cancel → tells the customer you need to adjust
  • "Tue 2pm instead" → counter-offer to the customer

Toggle auto-confirm

Set RIKER_AUTO_CONFIRM=true in Vercel env. Then Riker auto-books when the slot cleanly fits. You still get a heads-up text. Turn it off anytime by changing the value back.

What Riker Remembers (and Doesn't)

Does remember

  • Anything you tag as a standing order (priority 10, forever)
  • Customer preferences, relationships, equipment notes when the AI decides they're worth remembering
  • Pending items with expirations ("send insurance docs by May 1")
  • Route tips — gate codes, back entrance, "daughter translates"
  • Your full custody schedule + work hours

Doesn't remember

  • Pattern-mining your behavior on its own (if you reject 3 Thursdays, it won't auto-infer a rule — tell it to make a rule)
  • Anything from conversations older than 30 days at priority < 5
  • Corrections unless you phrase them as a rule
  • Anything after a failed memory write (it logs errors — check riker_interactions)

When Things Go Sideways

Kill switches (Vercel env vars)

  • RIKER_AUTO_CONFIRM = false forces every booking through your approval
  • Remove the cron entries in vercel.json to pause proactive behaviors
  • Change Twilio's inbound webhook URL to disable SMS
  • Remove the Gmail "Riker" filter to disable email

Audit trails

  • riker_interactions — every Claude call, cost, actions taken
  • pending_confirmations — every proposed action, whether approved
  • messages + conversations — every SMS/email turn
  • mazon_audit_log — every Mazon state change

Mazon Quick Rules

  • Customer needs a phone on billing account AND Mazon-approved flag before you can tap Mazon.
  • Batch won't ship below $1,000.
  • Ship by 10 AM CT Friday for same-day funding.
  • Invoice flows: factored_pending → factored_submitted → paid. Never hits paid until Mazon actually funds.
  • If Mazon rejects, invoice goes back to sent with a red banner — collect another way.
