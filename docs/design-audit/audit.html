01 Executive summary

The product works. The design is actively fighting it.

Stephens Advanced has built an impressive operational platform — 18,000+ systems under management, a real field app, a real portal, a real AI assistant. The functionality is ahead of most competitors in this vertical. But the visual and interaction design across these surfaces is a patchwork of three half-systems, two themes, one chat widget that no technician will talk to, and a field UI that punishes the person it's supposed to help. None of this is unfixable. Most of it is a week of disciplined design work away from being genuinely good.
